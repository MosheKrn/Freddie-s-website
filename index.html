Q: Describe what steps to take to search the site "Paracetamol Side Effects" in WEB1, WEB2 and WEB3

WEB 1.0 WEB 2.0 WEB 3.0
In WEB 1.0 the search is required to be very specific, so in order to search for paracetamol side effects I need the address of the site from which I want to get the information and then search for paracetamol and then look for its side effects In WEB 2.0 I can enter "paracetamol side effects" in Google for example and I will get sites that talk about this topic, you can also get feedback from people who have mentioned their information on these sites In WEB 3.0 the search can be very short and I will get very broad information, which means if I search for "paracetamol information effects" on Google I will get the information I need without trying to search for it in all the results I received
